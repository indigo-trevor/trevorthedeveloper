Who am I?

Hello everyone!

My name is Trevor Robinson and I am a creative developer based out of Seattle, Washington. I am currently employed as the UI Director at a Digital Marketing Agency called Indigo Slate.

While my roots are firmed planted as a full-stack developer, I have a serious obsession with all things UI/UX. If it doesn’t look good, feel good, and provide immediate and clear value… We will have problem.... Ok not a REAL problem...

I pride myself in always putting the user first. At the end-of-the-day, they are what really moves the needle.

My constant obsession with picking up and learning emerging technologies and practices leaves there never a dull moment.

I love to network and meet other crazy/unique people so if any of this is of interest to you, shoot me a note.
