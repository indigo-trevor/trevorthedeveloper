Who am I?

Hello everyone!

My name is Trevor Robinson and I am a creative developer based out of Seattle, Washington. During the week, most of my time is spent creating digital experiences with my extremely talented colleagues over at Indigo Slate where I am the UI Director.

While my roots are planted as a full-stack developer, I have an obsession with all things UI/UX. I think we can all appreciate things that look good, feel good, and provide immediate and clear value... Ammirite?

I find myself asking this question in pretty much everything I do: How can this experience provide the most value to the user? I have found that the process of answering this question is my true passion.

My boundless curiosity leaves never a dull moment. I find myself on the never-ending quest to pick other people brains, far smarter than I, and learn emerging technologies and practices.

If any of this is of interest to you, shoot me a note.
